Linking course sections

Before we begin, you will want to link the concurrent sections of your courses together. This will save some headache when setting up your grade book, it will also allow for shortcuts when you are doing your grading.

  • Detailed Instructions
  • Video Instructions

First from the Course tab go to See All from here you click on the settings symbol () for a course section you would like to link. Then the other sections you would like to link to it.

Caution

Only link sections at the beginning of the year. When sections are linked, data from one of the sections will be lost. Never unlink sections mid year, as gradebook data will be lost with for the linked section.

Once linked, material added to the course will be added to all sections. There are options to make section specific material.

Building a folder structure

A well organized materials section for your course will help students to navigate the course content.

Standard Expectation

All courses must have a course outline at the top of the materials. This will help parents in understanding what information to find where. Although different classes will understanbly have different folder structures, this document should be considered the best practice.
